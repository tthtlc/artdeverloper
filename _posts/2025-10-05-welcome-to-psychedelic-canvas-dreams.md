Welcome to **Psychedelic Canvas Dreams** — a space for exploring generative art, interactive canvas experiments, and visual mathematics.

## What to Expect

This site is a living sketchbook of algorithmic art. Each post pairs an interactive HTML5 Canvas with notes on the math and code behind it.

## How to Explore

Every post here is self-contained — open any one and you'll find a live, interactive piece you can play with directly in the browser. No installs, no dependencies.

Check back for new experiments, or browse the [posts archive](/posts/) to see everything so far.
